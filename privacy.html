Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

At AuditExport, we take a "Zero-Knowledge" approach to your data. Because our software runs locally on your infrastructure, we do not have access to your source code, your tokens, or your generated reports.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect:

  • Your GitHub Personal Access Tokens (PATs).
  • Your source code or repository contents.
  • The audit evidence artifacts generated by the tool.

We do collect:

  • Payment information (processed securely via our authorized payment partners).
  • Email addresses provided during the purchase process for license delivery.

2. Data Usage

The software executes entirely within your environment (local machine or CI/CD runner). All API calls to GitHub are made directly from your IP address to GitHub's servers. No data is proxied through AuditExport servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use authorized payment partners as our payment processor and Merchant of Record. When you purchase a license, you are subject to their Privacy Policy regarding payment data handling.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact support@auditexport.com.
